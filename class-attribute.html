For a one-year period from November 2010, the Marugame Genichiro-Inokuma Museum of Contemporary Art (MIMOCA) will host a cycle of four Hiroshi Sugimoto exhibition

Each will showcase works by the artist thematically contextualized under the headings "Science," "Architecture," "History" and "Religion" so to present a comprehensive panorama of the artist's oeuvre.

Hours: 10:00 - 18:00 (No admittance after 17:30)
